Introducing Aether

Aether is a product that projects holograms of items from the computer onto air, and allows data to be manipulated through gestures using the Kinect and a projector.

Latest Updates

February 19, 2013

Website is nearly complete, and prototype is being built.

Motivation

We want to create a way to interact with a computer without having to use a keyboard and mouse. Gestures are much more natural to humans, and can also help people with disabilities interact with computers. People should be able control a computer just through the hologram.
